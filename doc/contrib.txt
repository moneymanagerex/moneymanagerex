People have different privacy concerns: our goal is to be clear about what information we collect, so that you can make meaningful choices about how it is used.

When starting and using  Money Manager EX application, we might collect ANONYMOUS usage statistics like version, geo-location, language, platform and most used functions. No private or financial information is collected.
 
2. What do we use your information for?
Any of the information we collect from you may be used in one of the following ways: make statistics about software usage and distribution; personalize your experience (your information helps us to better respond to your individual needs); improve our application (we continually improve our offerings based on the information and feedback we receive from you)
 
3. Do we disclose any information to outside parties?
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties collected information.
 
4. Disable statistics sending
You can turn of at any moment sending of stats simply removing corresponding flag under Options->Network.
 
5. Your consent

6. Changes to our Privacy policy
